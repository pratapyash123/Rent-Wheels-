Mini Cooper

Small, sporty, and stylish

The Mini Cooper is an iconic compact car known for its agile handling, quirky design, and premium features. It’s a fun-to-drive car that excels in urban environments, offering a stylish ride with excellent fuel efficiency.

Engine

The Mini Cooper comes with a 1.5L, 3-cylinder turbocharged engine that produces around 134 horsepower and 220 Nm of torque, providing a lively driving experience.

Transmission

It features a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission, allowing for smooth shifts and responsive performance on both city streets and highways.

Performance

The Mini Cooper can go from 0 to 100 km/h in around 7 seconds, with a top speed of 205 km/h, making it quick and fun to drive while maintaining excellent fuel efficiency.

Dimensions

The Mini Cooper has a length of 3821 mm, a width of 1727 mm, and a height of 1414 mm, with a wheelbase of 2495 mm, making it compact yet comfortable for everyday use.

Interior

The interior of the Mini Cooper is stylish and well-equipped, with leather upholstery, an 8.8-inch infotainment system, and premium sound systems available in higher trims.
