Mini Cooper SE

The stylish electric compact car

The Mini Cooper SE is the all-electric version of the iconic Mini Cooper. Combining fun driving dynamics with zero emissions, the Mini Cooper SE offers an eco-friendly option without compromising style or performance.

Electric Motor

The Mini Cooper SE is powered by a 32.6 kWh lithium-ion battery, paired with a 184 horsepower electric motor that delivers 270 Nm of torque. This provides excellent acceleration and an enjoyable driving experience.

Transmission

Equipped with a single-speed automatic transmission, the Mini Cooper SE offers smooth and responsive power delivery, ideal for city driving.

Performance

The Mini Cooper SE can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 7.3 seconds, with a top speed of 150 km/h. It offers a nimble and responsive performance in urban environments.

Range

With a full charge, the Mini Cooper SE offers an estimated range of around 230-250 km, depending on driving conditions and usage.

Dimensions

The Mini Cooper SE has a length of 3821 mm, a width of 1727 mm, and a height of 1414 mm, making it compact and perfect for tight city spaces.

Interior

The interior features a premium design with sustainable materials, a 6.5-inch infotainment display, and available leather upholstery for an eco-friendly yet luxurious feel.
