Mini Countryman

The stylish and spacious compact SUV

The Mini Countryman is a compact SUV that blends the brand's iconic style with practicality and versatility. It offers a spacious interior, advanced technology, and impressive performance, all in a compact, city-friendly package.

Engine Options

The Mini Countryman is available with multiple engine options, including a 1.5L 3-cylinder engine, a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine, and a plug-in hybrid variant. The engines provide a balance of power and efficiency, making it ideal for both urban and off-road driving.

Transmission

The Countryman comes with a 6-speed manual or an 8-speed automatic transmission, depending on the model, delivering a smooth and responsive driving experience.

Performance

The Mini Countryman offers excellent acceleration, with the 2.0L engine delivering 189 horsepower. The car can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 7.5 seconds, with a top speed of 225 km/h.

Dimensions

The Mini Countryman measures 4299 mm in length, 1822 mm in width, and 1557 mm in height. It has a wheelbase of 2670 mm, providing ample interior space for passengers and cargo.

Interior

The Countryman offers a premium interior with high-quality materials, a 6.5-inch or 8.8-inch infotainment display, and available leather upholstery. The car seats up to five passengers, with ample legroom and cargo space for a comfortable journey.

Safety Features

The Mini Countryman is equipped with numerous safety features, including front and side airbags, rearview camera, parking sensors, lane departure warning, and more. It also offers an optional all-wheel-drive system for enhanced stability and control.

Fuel Efficiency

The Countryman offers a fuel efficiency of approximately 13-16 km/l, depending on the engine and driving conditions.

Weight
