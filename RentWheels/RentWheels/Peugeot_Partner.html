Peugeot Partner

Reliable and versatile for all your commercial needs

The Peugeot Partner is a compact yet spacious van designed for both business and leisure. Whether you need it for transporting goods or family trips, it provides excellent performance and practicality.

Engine

The Partner comes with a choice of petrol and diesel engines, offering efficient fuel consumption and smooth performance. It boasts a 1.6L engine with 100 hp and a 1.5L diesel variant with 130 hp.

Transmission

It features a 5-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission for smooth driving, ideal for both city and highway journeys.

Capacity

With up to 4.4m³ of cargo space, the Partner can easily handle your business or personal transport needs. Its payload capacity reaches up to 850 kg depending on the model.

Performance

Thanks to its efficient engine options, the Peugeot Partner offers excellent fuel economy and can go from 0 to 100 km/h in just 10.6 seconds (for the 1.5L version).

Dimensions

The Partner measures 4380 mm in length, 1810 mm in width, and 1860 mm in height, making it compact yet spacious enough for various transport tasks.

Models

The Peugeot Partner comes in multiple variants including the standard model and an electric version (Peugeot Partner Electric), designed for eco-conscious businesses.
