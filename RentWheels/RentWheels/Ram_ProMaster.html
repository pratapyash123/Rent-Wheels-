Ram ProMaster

Perfect for your commercial and cargo needs

The Ram ProMaster is a versatile and reliable van ideal for businesses, cargo transportation, and large families who require maximum space and comfort.

Engine

The ProMaster is powered by a 3.6L V6 engine that generates 280 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering a perfect balance between power and efficiency for heavy-duty tasks.

Transmission

It features a 6-speed automatic transmission for smooth shifting and efficiency during both city and highway driving.

Capacity

With a cargo space of up to 462 cubic feet, the ProMaster is perfect for transporting large items. It can carry a payload of up to 4,680 lbs depending on the model.

Performance

The ProMaster can handle a variety of driving conditions, whether urban roads or highways, with its front-wheel-drive configuration providing excellent traction.

Dimensions

The ProMaster measures 236 inches in length, 100.8 inches in width, and 90.5 inches in height, making it one of the largest cargo vans on the market.

Models

The ProMaster is available in various models, including 1500, 2500, and 3500, catering to different commercial needs and payload requirements.
