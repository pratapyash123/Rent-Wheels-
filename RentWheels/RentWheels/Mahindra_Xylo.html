Mahindra Xylo

Comfort and space for the entire family

The Mahindra Xylo is a versatile and spacious MPV designed for family comfort and long drives. It offers ample space and a smooth ride, making it perfect for both city and highway driving.

Engine

The Xylo is powered by a 2.5L mDI CRDe engine that produces 112 bhp and 240 Nm of torque, offering a smooth driving experience with solid performance.

Transmission

The vehicle comes with a 5-speed manual transmission for easy handling and efficient driving.

Capacity

The Xylo offers seating for up to 8 passengers, providing ample space for families or groups traveling together.

Performance

With a top speed of 150 km/h and good acceleration, the Xylo is a solid performer for long-distance travel and city commutes.

Dimensions

The Xylo measures 4520 mm in length, 1850 mm in width, and 1915 mm in height, making it a spacious and comfortable ride for passengers and luggage.

Models

The Mahindra Xylo is available in multiple variants, including the E4, E8, and the top-spec E9, with different features and trim levels for various preferences.

Rent the Mahindra Xylo for a family vacation, road trips, or group outings!

Fuel tank

The Xylo has a 55-liter fuel tank, providing a good driving range for long trips.

Weight

The Xylo has a gross vehicle weight (GVW) of approximately 2,500 kg, depending on the model variant.
